---

## Lessons Learned

1. **Architecture > Code** - The Python script was too concrete too early
2. **Multi-AI synthesis works** - Different models caught different gaps  
3. **Philosophy matters** - "Bones not Chains" guided every decision
4. **Iteration compounds** - Each version built on the last without pivoting
5. **Vision persists** - With consistent human coordination, AI collaboration produces coherent results
